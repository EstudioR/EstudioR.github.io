Funciones importantes en lattice

Diagrama de cajas (Boxplot)

bwplot(factor(datConsumo$A√±o) ~ datConsumo$Consumo,
       col = 2,
       xlab = "Consumo de proteina animal",
       ylab = "A√±o")

bwplot(factor(datConsumo$Item) ~ datConsumo$Consumo,
       col = 2,
       xlab = "Consumo de proteina animal",
       ylab = "item de consumo")

Diagrama de barras

barchart(factor(datConsumo$A√±o) ~ datConsumo$Consumo,
         col = "blue",
         xlab = "Consumo de prote√≠na animal",
         ylab = "A√±o")

Consumo por item y a√±o:

barchart(factor(datConsumo$A√±o) ~ datConsumo$Consumo
         | factor(datConsumo$Item),
         col = "forestgreen",
         xlab = "Consumo de prote√≠na animal",
         ylab = "A√±o")

Consumo por a√±o e item:
